What's HFP? Well, Hope for Paws are a group, non-profit, who help and rescue homeless animals and help them having a new life.
You can help them too, by helping the homeless animals or your local associations with money, food, medicine, bathing products and of course, loving your pet!
Do not be like the ones that abandon their loyal friends...! Remember, you may have more than one friend(pet) in your life but for your friend(pet) you are his whole life!
